About Us

WE ARE LEADER IN THIRD PARTY LOGISTICS

Lawrence Victor Companies is a full service supply chain logistics company based in Kabul. As a leading 3PL logistics company, we are one of the largest and most trusted in the Upper Midwest, serving more than 250 customers that range from Fortune 500 to start-up companies

Our services include
warehousing, Transportation, fulfillment, customized logistics, and international services. We started in 1904 with two horses and a wagon, and we are extremely proud of our history. Since then, we have grown to operate 15 facilities with a total of 4.2million square feet of bulk, rack, and food grade storage and employ over 330 peopele throughout Mnnesota, Iowa, and Missouri. As 3PL experts, we offer flexibility to meet your unique business needs. We are resourceful and focused on providing the best warehousing and logistics solutions.
